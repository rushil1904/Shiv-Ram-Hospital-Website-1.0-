Message from the Director



Healthcare in our country is yet to reach the least privileged sections of the society. The main aim of setting up this hospital in a tier 3 city is to bridge the gap between primary care set up on one side and the tertiary corporate hospitals on the other, which remains beyond the reach of a common man’s budget. Providing the quality health care within the common man’s budget at affordable cost remains the main aim of our hospital. We look forward to giving high quality healthcare at reasonable rates and homely atmosphere.
