Shiv Ram Hospital located on Hailey Mandi Road, Opposite West Academy School, Pataudi. Hospital is maintaining 30 patient beds which includes two bedded ICU and two private rooms. We are here to provide affordable care to the people living in the vicinity of 10-15 kms of Pataudi so that they avoid going to corporate hospitals and can get treatment at a very reasonable cost. We are providing care for all cardiology cases, chest diseases, dengue, malaria, HIV/AIDS and other areas of clinical medicine. We also provide counselling on various life style diseases.We are committed to contribute to the physical, psychological, social and spiritual well being of the patients and the community. We have got a very comprehensive setup which includes OPD services, pharmacy and good laboratory facilities.
